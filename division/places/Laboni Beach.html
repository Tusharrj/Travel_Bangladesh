Laboni Beach

Laboni Beach is one of the widest and busiest beaches of Asia.Perhaps the most popular beach in Cox Bazar and infact in the whole of Bangladesh – and the most popular day of the year i.e 31st Dec night/ 1st Jan; togetherly made an extravagant affair. There were people all over the beach…. Lights, music, fun-frolic, food, fish, fair and the colourful beach carnivalfor 3 consecutive nights. If you are camping at Laboni take ½ a days outing to Kolatoli beach and Inani beach in the south and further south. Also do spend half a day for shopping in the Burmese market, which has a good spread of textiles and pickles. Here’s to test your bargaining skills. If you are a dry-fish lover you’ll get the widest collection of the world. And never afford to miss the most beautiful sunset upon Bay of Bengal.
